Bubble Game Instructions



1. You have a total of 60 seconds to play the game.

2. Your task is to locate and press the bubble that matches the Hit value.

3. Upon successfully hitting the correct bubble, your score will increase by 10, and all bubbles, along with the Hit value, will be refreshed.

4. If you hit the wrong bubble, nothing will change.

5. When you're ready, press the START button


Best of luck!!!
